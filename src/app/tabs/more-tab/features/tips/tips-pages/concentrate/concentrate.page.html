Concentrate

How it works:

Focusing on a single task or thought can help reduce anxiety and stress. Techniques such as "one-task-at-a-time" or focusing on an object or thought can improve concentration.

Why it works:

Concentrating on a single task prevents your mind from becoming overwhelmed by competing thoughts, which reduces cognitive load and enhances problem-solving abilities. Concentration also helps reduce rumination, a common feature of anxiety and depression, by redirecting attention from negative thoughts.
