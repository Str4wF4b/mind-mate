Meditation & Mindfulness

How it works:

Meditation and mindfulness help focus attention on the present moment and calm racing thoughts.

Why it works:

These techniques promote stress reduction and can enhance brain activity in areas responsible for emotional regulation. Mindfulness can also reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression.

Kabat-Zinn, J. (2003). Mindfulness-based stress reduction (MBSR). Constructivism in the Human Sciences, 8(2), 73-85.
Goyal, M., et al. (2014). Meditation programs for psychological stress and well-being: A systematic review and meta-analysis. JAMA Internal Medicine, 174(3), 357-368.
