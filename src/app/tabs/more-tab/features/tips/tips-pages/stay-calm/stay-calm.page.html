Stay Calm

How it works:

Staying calm involves consciously controlling your reactions in stressful situations. This can be achieved through techniques such as deep breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, or counting to ten before responding.

Why it works:

When you stay calm, you activate the parasympathetic nervous system, which helps lower the heart rate and reduce stress hormones like cortisol. This reaction can prevent the fight-or-flight response that occurs in high-stress situations, helping to maintain mental clarity and emotional stability.
