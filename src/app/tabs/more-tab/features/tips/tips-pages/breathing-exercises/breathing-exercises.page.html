Breathing Exercises

How it works:

Breathing exercises help calm the mind and relax the body. They may involve simple, controlled breathing techniques like deep inhalation and exhalation.

Why it works:

Breathing exercises activate the parasympathetic nervous system, which is responsible for rest and digestion. This helps slow down the heart rate and relax the muscles, reducing stress and anxiety.
