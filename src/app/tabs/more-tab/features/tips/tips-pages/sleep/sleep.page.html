Getting Sleep

How it works:

Quality sleep is essential for mental health. It allows the brain to process emotions and restore cognitive functions.

Why it works:

Sleep regulates mood, helps in the processing of memories, and reduces emotional reactivity. Poor sleep is strongly linked to mental health issues such as anxiety and depression. Ensuring sufficient sleep helps stabilize mood, cognitive performance, and emotional well-being.

Walker, M. P. (2017). Sleep loss and the brain. Neuropsychopharmacology, 45(1), 74-89.
