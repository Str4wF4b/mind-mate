Breathing Fresh Air

How it works:

Spending time outdoors can improve your overall mood and enhance well-being. Fresh air and nature can help with relaxation.

Why it works:

Fresh air and physical activity in nature can lower stress hormones such as cortisol. Additionally, natural sunlight boosts Vitamin D production, which has positive effects on mood. Physical activity also stimulates endorphin production, acting as a natural mood enhancer.

Barton, J., & Pretty, J. (2010). What is the best dose of nature and green exercise for mental health? A multi-study analysis. Environmental Science & Technology, 44(10), 3947-3955.
Sullivan, W. C., et al. (2004). Beyond green space: Assessing the health benefits of nature. The Journal of Environmental Psychology, 24(3), 271-278.
