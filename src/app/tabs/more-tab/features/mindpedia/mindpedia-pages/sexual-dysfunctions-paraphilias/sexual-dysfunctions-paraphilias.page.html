Sexual Dysfunction & Paraphilias

What is it?

Sexual dysfunctions include problems with sexual response, desire, or orgasm. Paraphilic disorders involve atypical sexual interests that may cause distress or harm. Gender dysphoria should also be mentioned here. It refers to the distress some people feel due to a mismatch between their gender identity and assigned sex.

Symptoms

  • Lack of sexual interest
  • Inability to achieve arousal
  • Discomfort with one's body and gender role

Possible Treatment

Therapies (CBT or Sex Therapy):


  • Addressing emotional and psychological aspects

Hormonal Treatments or Surgeries:


  • For gender dysphoria
